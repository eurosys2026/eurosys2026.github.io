Workshops

Workshops co-organized with EuroSys 2021:

  • 15th EuroSys Doctoral Workshop (EuroDW)

  • 1st European Workshop on Machine Learning and Systems (EuroMLSys)
  • 14th European Workshop on Systems Security (EUROSEC)
  • 8th Workshop on Principles and Practice of Consistency for Distributed Data (PaPoC)
  • 11th Workshop on Systems for Post-Moore Architectures (SPMA)
  • Workshop on Challenges and Opportunities of Efficient and Performant Storage Systems (CHEOPS)
  • 12th Workshop on Large-Scale Distributed Systems and Middleware (LADIS)
  • 1st Workshop on High Availability and Observability of Cloud Systems (HAOC)

    • All workshops will be held on Monday, 26th April 2021.
