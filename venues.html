Transportation & Parking

Public Transport

  • Tram: The nearest tram stop is Haymarket Station, approximately 5 minutes on foot.
  • Rail: Edinburgh Waverley, the city's main railway station, is approximately 15 minutes on foot.
  • Bus: St Andrews Square bus terminal, served by local and regional routes, is approximately 20 minutes on foot.

Parking

  • Several NCP car parks are within close walking distance of the EICC.
  • Sheraton Hotel Car Park (adjacent to the venue).
  • Semple Street Car Park (nearby).
  • Spaces can also be booked online via YourParkingSpace.
