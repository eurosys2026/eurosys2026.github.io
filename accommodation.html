The conference negotiated hotel room rates are available on this page.

The information on this page is for your convenience, please refer to the reservation web page for the definitive rates, conditions and information.

Edinburgh has a wide range of accommodation options to suit all budgets. We recommend booking early, especially for the conference dates (April 27–30, 2026). Options include hotels and Airbnb.

Walking distances are given from both the Conference Venue (EICC) and the Banquet Venue (NMS). The two venues are approximately 17 minutes apart on foot.
